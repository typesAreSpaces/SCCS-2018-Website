University of New Mexico - Spring 2018

Computer Science Student Conference (CSSC) is a student-organized conference series that has traditionally presented new developments in computer science, as well as the latest research directions within the Computer Science department at UNM. This year we aim to showcase UNM Research to the New Mexico Community at large.

Authors are invited to submit original or previously published manuscripts on a wide range of computer science areas, including (but not limited to) algorithms, systems, HPC, cybersecurity, robotics, non-conventional computing, data science, and applications. For more information, please refer to the Call for Papers.
